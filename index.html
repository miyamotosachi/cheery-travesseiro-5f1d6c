The 7-Day Sprint

Day 1: Intake & Strategy

Deep dive interview. We define your cultural positioning and "enemy" in the market.

Day 2-3: AI Ideation

We generate 500+ concepts using MidJourney v7 and proprietary prompts. Raw volume.

Day 4: Human Curation

Senior Designer selects top 5 concepts. Applies emotional intelligence and cultural nuance.

Day 5-6: Refinement

We refine the winner. Create variations, test legibility, and prepare assets for web/print.

Day 7: Delivery & Handoff

You receive the Brand Bible, Figma files, and a "Launch Pack" ready to deploy.

Ongoing: Support

30 days of minor tweaks included. We ensure the brand lands perfectly in the real world.
